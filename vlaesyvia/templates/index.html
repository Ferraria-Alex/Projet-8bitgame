Introduction :

In Vlaesyvia, players forge heroes to battle through story-driven levels where typing prowess becomes combat. Each character can wield four abilities either general or class-specific ones. Earn currency by defeating waves of enemies to unlock new abilities in the in-game store, where class-specific and universal upgrades await. Track your arsenal in the Codex — a visual inventory showing unlocked powers and locked ones. Compete globally in the endless mode surviving ever-stronger waves. Connect an account to save progress, create multiple heroes, and rise through the ranks.

World story :

Humanity's progress has been trapped in a cruel cycle—each golden age shattered by cataclysms that plunge civilization back into ruin. After the latest collapse, survivors unearthed Vahter, a volatile elemental force that bends nature itself. To harness it, the Adventurer’s Guild arose: a coalition of Vahter-wielders called the Vahter-Touched, tasked with protecting humanity from the monstrous aberrations spawned by each new disaster. But over time, the Guild lost its way. What was once a group devoted to the people became controlled by the rich and powerful, the nobles who now use it for their own gain. As monstrous hordes surge, most skilled adventurers refuse to serve the Guild’s corrupted interests. Instead, they fight alone, defending their homes with scavenged Vahter relics and raw ingenuity.
